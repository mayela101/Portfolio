Problem Statement

While journaling is widely recognized as a powerful tool for improving mental wellness, many individuals struggle to maintain a consistent practice. Common challenges include blank-page anxiety, uncertainty about what to write, and difficulty reflecting on past entries to identify meaningful emotional patterns over time.


As a result, journaling often becomes a passive log of daily events rather than an active tool for growth.


Bloom was designed to address these challenges by creating a private, empathetic journaling companion that encourages consistency, and transforms journaling into a fun and rewarding reflective habit.


Target Users

  • Individuals focused on mental wellness and self-reflection
  • People new to journaling who need structure and encouragement
  • Busy professionals seeking a quick, effective way to process their day

Solution

Bloom is an AI-assisted journaling application that combines:

  • Guided, context-aware prompts
  • Lightweight emotional analysis
  • Visual progress metaphors

The system is intentionally designed so that AI supports the user without thinking for them or criticizing their actions.

Insights are reflective rather than prescriptive, and progress is framed as personal growth rather than performance or comparison.

Core Features

  • Guided journaling with optional mood tagging
  • Context aware AI-generated prompts
  • Weekly emotional insights dashboard
  • Progress visualization using butterfly metamorphosis
  • Local fallback analysis when AI services are unavailable
